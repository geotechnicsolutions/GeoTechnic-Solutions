Who we are

Geoscience roots, transition mindset

Geotechnic Solutions was founded by a geoscientist with a passion for the energy transition. We blend decades of subsurface expertise with modern GIS and AI to help you make better decisions, faster.

Why this exists

Closing the subsurface knowledge gap

The green transition is being slowed down by uncertainty in the subsurface: geothermal potential that isn’t mapped, critical minerals that are poorly understood, and decisions that take years instead of months. We exist to shorten that path.

Traditional project development often jumps straight into costly drilling or exploration without adequate desktop screening. Our mission is to provide a clear, affordable picture of what lies beneath before committing capital. By fusing classical geology with advanced GIS and AI, we deliver insights that de‑risk projects and accelerate timelines.

Our approach

Geoscience meets technology

We’re not just data wranglers. We’re interpreters of the subsurface. Our approach combines human expertise with automation to give you the best of both worlds.

Data collection and modelling are only part of the story. Interpretation and contextualisation are where our expertise comes in. We use AI to accelerate the routine work, freeing our team to focus on critical decisions and nuanced insights.

Team & partners

Working globally from the Netherlands

Based in the Tech Start‑Up Hub Haarlem and registered with the Dutch Chamber of Commerce (KvK), Geotechnic Solutions operates globally through a trusted network of geoscientists, engineers and analysts.

We partner with domain experts in drilling, reservoir engineering, environmental permitting and finance to build multidisciplinary teams tailored to your project. Our remote‑first workflows ensure efficient collaboration wherever your project is located.
