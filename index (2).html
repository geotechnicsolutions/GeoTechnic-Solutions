Process

From polygon to decision in a few clear steps

Whether you’re a municipality, a developer, or an investor, we keep the process lean: a focused desktop assessment first, then only the deeper studies that are actually needed.

2

About

Geoscience roots, transition mindset

Geotechnic Solutions was founded by a geoscientist with a passion for the energy transition, bringing together subsurface expertise, GIS, and AI to bridge the gap between raw data and real-world projects.

Why this exists

The green transition is being slowed down by uncertainty in the subsurface: geothermal potential that isn’t mapped, critical minerals that are poorly understood, and decisions that take years instead of months.

Geotechnic Solutions exists to shorten that path. By combining classical geology and geophysics with modern GIS and AI tooling, we help you quickly understand where it makes sense to invest time, money, and attention – and where it doesn’t.

Based in the Netherlands, working globally.
