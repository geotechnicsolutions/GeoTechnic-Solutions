Our method

From polygon to decision

We keep it lean and decision‑oriented. Our process is designed to maximise insight and minimise cost at the early stages of your project.

Step 1

Share your area & ambition

Provide a polygon, site boundary or regional area. Tell us what you’re considering – geothermal heating, wind/solar, strategic minerals – plus any constraints like budget, timeline or risk appetite.

We start by understanding your goals and constraints. A well‑defined geographic scope and high‑level vision enable us to tailor the screening to your needs.

Step 2

AI+GIS desktop analysis

We assemble and harmonise existing subsurface and surface data. Our AI‑assisted workflows rank prospectivity, overlay constraints and draft narratives for your report.

Using open data, geological maps, heat‑flow models, satellite imagery and more, we build a holistic view of the subsurface. Machine‑learning models and rule‑based scoring highlight areas of promise and flag risks. Our geoscientists then validate and refine the results.

Step 3

Decision‑ready outputs

We deliver concise reports with maps, key metrics and recommended next steps – ready to share with colleagues, boards or investors.

Depending on the package, you will receive a 10–40 page report and an optional slide deck. These include prospectivity maps, techno‑economic metrics and a roadmap for further de‑risking and development.
