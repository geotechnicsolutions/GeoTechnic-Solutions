Rare Earth Elements for the Green Transition

Overview of key REEs, their uses, geological occurrence, and economic parameters

Neodymium (Nd)

Applications: Permanent magnets in EVs, turbines, robotics, and headphones
Geological Occurrence: Monazite, bastnäsite, and lateritic clays in carbonatite-hosted deposits
Economic Parameters: 100–300 kg/ha | ~$80–$150/kg (2025 est.)

Dysprosium (Dy)

Applications: High-temp magnets in EV motors, nuclear control rods
Geological Occurrence: Ion-adsorption clays, bastnäsite, and xenotime
Economic Parameters: 20–60 kg/ha | ~$280–$450/kg
