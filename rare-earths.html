What Are Rare Earths?

Rare earth elements (REEs) include 15 lanthanides plus yttrium (Y) and scandium (Sc). These critical elements are essential to the green and digital transitions. They power electric vehicles, wind turbines, lasers, AI chips, sensors, and more.
